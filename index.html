About

VITeach is a student-run social outreach club operating from the VIT Chennai. VITeach was founded with the sole motive of making a difference in the society, and we ordained upon the field of education to pursue its activities. VITeach started out in 2013 in partnership with Adi Dravida Welfare (ADW) Middle School, Melakkottaiyur, and to this day, the school has remained the club’s primary project. The club has a strength of 161 inspired engineering students from across all branches, coming together with the unified motive of uplifting young minds through education.

ADW Middle School

The primary project of VITeach is to help provide a better standard of education to the underprivileged children at the ADW Middle School situated in Melakkottaiyur. VITeach had its first official school visit on 31st August 2013. Seventeen club members visited the government-run school for the first ice-breaking session held that day. Since then, VITeach has been teaching English for students of grades 1 – 8 on a daily basis. At present, the students are taught English and Mathematics by a group of VITeachers visiting the school on every working day.

Angilam Express

On the invitation of the District Collector of Thiruvannamalai, VITeach conducted spoken English and life skill training at Aanaipiranthaan Middle School, located in the outskirts of the district.  This project was christened “Angilam Express”, and five sessions were conducted at the school over weekends. These sessions included means to improve on their vocabulary, diction, and their overall ability to converse in English, while also imparting the children with an insight into various educational and career options available as a means of exposing them to a world beyond what they see.

Naik Foundation

Naik foundation is a Maharashtra based NGO which provides more than 3 lakh mid-day meals per day to kids in Maharashtra and Gujarat. VITeach collaborated with Naik Foundation to develop an algorithm that will make their delivery system more efficient. The algorithm was implemented into their daily schedule and ensured timely delivery of the food.

Wish Tree and Book Drive

The book drive initiative was held during VIT Chennai’s cultural festival, Vibrance. Provisions were made at the portico for students and faculties to donate books for setting up a library at ADW Middle School. A vendor selling children’s books at Rs. 50/- each was identified and given a space on the college grounds, where students could buy books and donate the same. The Wish Tree was a parallel initiative that intended to fulfil a few little wishes of our kids at the ADW Middle School, in the form of material needs like pencils, colours, and the like. A total of 352 children’s books and 92 engineering books worth an approximate amount of Rs. 65,000/- were collected from the initiative. The engineering books collected will be exchanged for children’s books, and the books will be used to set up a library at the ADW Middle School.

Kumizhi Project

VITeach has commenced a weekend programme at GHS Kumizhi, where students of classes 6 to 10 are coached for examinations like NTSE, NMMSE, Olympiads, and the like. The students are also guided and assisted in building and presenting models at science fairs across the country.
