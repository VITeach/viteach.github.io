About Viteach

Viteach is all about improving the lives of future champs and other organizations helping them by providing teachers and infrastructure to a educated future.

ADW Primary School

Healthy infrastructure, sufficient stationary and memorable edutainment days are the basics in order to provide proper education. Viteach works with ADW primary school to provide the basic needs to these little champs for their better educated future.

Angilam Express

Angilam express is taking a step forward ,to educate the middle school students on Communicative English and Life Skills launching at Aanaipiranthan Middle School, Thiruvannamalai. Providing a better future to the students of Thiruvannamalai district.

Naik Foundation

Naik Foundation goals to reach billion lives globally through their initiatives in area of nutrition and meals , Viteach is working with them on an algorithm in order to simplify and increase the efficiency of delivery.

Kumizhi Project
